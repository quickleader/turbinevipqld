## Why Indonesia Needs Flexible Boilers
Indonesia's industrial growth is concentrated in palm oil, sugar, pulp & paper, textile, and cement. Each sector produces combustible residue — empty fruit bunches, bagasse, bark, and kiln off-gas — that is best burned in a dedicated biomass or waste-heat boiler rather than discarded. A boiler manufacturer in Indonesia must therefore offer fuel flexibility and compliance with local emission standards. We engineer packages around your available fuel, not the other way around.

## Boiler Types We Supply
- **Biomass boilers** — fired on palm kernel shell, fibre, bagasse, and wood residue
- **Waste-heat recovery boilers (HRSG)** — capture gas-turbine exhaust for combined cycle and CHP
- **Utility steam generators** — for IPPs and grid-connected plants
- **Coal and multi-fuel boilers** — with modern emission control

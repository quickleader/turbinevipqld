## Product Range

### Condensing Steam Turbines

Optimized for maximum power generation efficiency. Exhaust steam is condensed in a surface condenser and returned to the boiler as feedwater. Ideal for dedicated power plants and base-load applications.

- **Small scale:** 3MW – 12MW
- **Medium scale:** 25MW – 50MW
- **Steam conditions:** 3.43MPa/435°C to 8.83MPa/535°C
- **Thermal efficiency:** Up to 85%

### Back-Pressure Steam Turbines

Designed for combined heat and power (CHP) applications. Exhaust steam is used directly for industrial process heating or district heating, achieving total energy utilization efficiency of over 80%.

- **Capacity range:** 500kW – 25MW
- **Exhaust pressure:** 0.3MPa – 1.6MPa (customizable)
- **Applications:** Sugar mills, paper mills, chemical plants, district heating systems

### Extraction Back-Pressure Steam Turbines

Combine power generation with adjustable steam extraction. Part of the incoming steam is extracted for process use while the remainder expands to condenser pressure, maximizing flexibility.

- **Extraction points:** 1–2 configurable stages
- **Extraction pressure:** 0.1MPa – 1.0MPa
- **Best for:** Industries with simultaneous power and heat demands

### Industrial Process Turbines

Specifically designed for harsh industrial environments — high temperature, high dust content fuels, biomass applications, and waste-to-energy plants.

- **Fuel flexibility:** Coal, biomass, municipal waste, tire pyrolysis gas
- **Design features:** Anti-corrosion materials, particulate-resistant blade profiles, robust sealing systems

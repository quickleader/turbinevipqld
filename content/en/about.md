## Company Profile

Established in year 2003, **Qingdao Quickleader International Trade Co., Ltd** provides EPC solutions for the core strategic sectors with a focus on steam turbine and power generation.

Combining over decades of design, engineering and manufacturing excellence, we have constantly evolved based on a vision of synergizing through our most critical asset — human wisdom and resource — accompanied by our domestic and international network of joint venture partners, providing a global focus on meeting and superseding the intrinsic and challenging needs of the core strategic energy sectors.

## The 4S Concept

We introduced the concept of the **automobile 4S service center** to the power plant industry — helping owners obtain energy more simply.

| 4S Pillar | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Sale** | EPC contract or main equipment supply |
| **Spare Parts** | Supply original spare parts accurately |
| **Service** | Engineering team maintains power plant on site |
| **Survey** | Annually defect inspection and report |
